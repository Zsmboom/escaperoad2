Stickman Racing

Game Instructions

About Stickman Racing

Stickman Racing is a free multiplayer parkour game where you jump, climb, slide, flip, swing, and run through obstacles. Avoid falling and reach the finish line! Challenge your friends or compete with players worldwide in this exciting parkour racing adventure.

What is Stickman Racing

This unique racing game combines parkour elements with competitive multiplayer gameplay. Each level presents new challenges and obstacles that will test your timing, reflexes, and parkour skills. Race against other players in real-time while performing amazing acrobatic moves!

How to Play

Master the Parkour

Success in Stickman Racing requires mastering various parkour moves and knowing when to use them. Time your jumps perfectly, maintain momentum through obstacles, and find the fastest route to the finish line.

Game Features

  • Multiplayer racing action
  • Various parkour moves
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Real-time competition
  • Progressive difficulty

Game Controls

  • Use A/D or Left/Right arrows to move
  • Press Space or Up Arrow to jump
  • Hold S or Down Arrow to slide
  • Press W near walls to wall jump
  • Double tap direction keys to dash

Highlighted Features

Tips and Strategies

  • Learn the perfect timing for jumps
  • Practice wall jumps for shortcuts

  • Study the course layout
  • Watch other players' techniques
