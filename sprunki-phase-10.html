Creative Gameplay

In Sprunki Phase 10, you'll adventure through vibrant environments like digital forests, mechanical cities, and fantastical geometric mazes. Every step and jump creates sounds that alter the scenery and reveal mysterious paths. Rhythm-based puzzles will challenge your rhythm skills and thinking ability.

As you progress deeper, the challenges become more complex with constantly moving platforms and changing walls. Some levels require you to race against time or explore secret passages to collect hidden items. Are you ready to master space and time in each rhythm?
