About Escape Road City 2

Escape Road City 2 is the exciting update to the popular action-escape game Escape Road City. Navigate challenging city roads, dodge obstacles, and master your skills to make a daring getaway. Take on new urban challenges in this thrilling sequel.

How to Play

  • Use A/D or Left/Right arrows to steer
  • Press W or Up Arrow to accelerate
  • Press S or Down Arrow to reverse
  • Navigate through busy city streets
  • Avoid traffic and urban obstacles
  • Plan your escape route carefully
