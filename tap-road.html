Game Instructions

About Tap Road

Tap Road is an interesting game released by Azgames.io. In the game, you will control a small ball rolling on a track full of obstacles. Your task is to control the ball so that it overcomes these obstacles and achieves the highest score. The gameplay is simple but requires high concentration and quick reflexes.

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Tap or click to control the ball
  • Time your taps carefully to avoid obstacles
  • Keep the ball on the track
  • Collect power-ups when available
