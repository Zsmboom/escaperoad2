About Sprunki

Play Sprunki Incredibox! Join the Incredibox community and create amazing music! With the Sprunki mod, you can mix music, share and connect with music lovers around the world.

What is Sprunki

Sprunki is an innovative music creation game based on the popular Incredibox platform. It allows you to become a music producer by mixing different beats, melodies, and sounds to create your own unique musical compositions. With its intuitive interface and vast sound library, anyone can create professional-sounding music!

How to Play

Create Your Music

The gameplay revolves around mixing and matching different musical elements to create harmonious compositions. Experiment with various sounds, rhythms, and melodies to discover your own unique style.
