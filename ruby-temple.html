Ruby Temple

Game Instructions

About Ruby Temple

Ruby Temple is an award-winning metroidvania platformer that won 3rd place in Scratch Game Jam #8! Embark on an exciting adventure as you explore mysterious temples, collect precious rubies, and uncover hidden secrets.

Game Features

  • Metroidvania Gameplay: Explore interconnected areas and discover new abilities
  • Ruby Collection: Find and collect precious rubies throughout the temple
  • Hidden Secrets: Uncover the mysteries of the temple
  • Original Soundtrack: Featuring music by Big Giant Circles

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move
  • Space to jump
  • E to pause the game

Game Objectives

  • Explore the mysterious temple
  • Collect all the rubies
  • Discover hidden areas
  • Unlock the temple's secrets

Tips for Success

  • Explore every corner of the temple
  • Look for hidden passages
  • Master the jumping mechanics
  • Remember to save your progress
  • Pay attention to visual clues

Special Features
