Sprunki Clicker

Game Instructions

About Sprunki Clicker

Sprunki Clicker is an endless clicker game that challenges you to create a Sprunki band by continuously tapping on the sprunki. Build your musical ensemble and watch it grow with each click!

What is Sprunki Clicker

This addictive clicker game combines music and rhythm with simple yet engaging gameplay mechanics. Each tap brings you closer to building the ultimate Sprunki band. The more you click, the bigger and better your band becomes!

How to Play

Click and Build

The gameplay is straightforward but engaging. Simply click or tap on the screen to add new elements to your Sprunki band. Watch as your musical ensemble grows and evolves with each interaction.
