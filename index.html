Escape Road 2

Game Instructions

About Escape Road 2

Escape Road 2 is an exciting driving game where you're in a thrilling police chase. Drive your car at high speeds to avoid capture while being pursued by police tanks, jeeps, and helicopters. The game features stunning 3D graphics and intense action, perfect for speed enthusiasts who want to showcase their driving skills.

How to Play

  • Use A/D or Left/Right arrows to steer
  • Press W or Up Arrow to accelerate
  • Press S or Down Arrow to reverse
  • Avoid obstacles and police vehicles
  • Don't get stuck for more than 3 seconds
  • Make quick turns and jump over obstacles to earn points
