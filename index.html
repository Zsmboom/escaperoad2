Welcome to Escape Road 2!

Escape Road is back with Escape Road 2! Get ready for the thrilling sequel that takes you on heart-pounding chasing and escaping gameplay! Your mission? Navigate the crowded roads, escape from the police, and survive! It's now updated with more vehicles, upgrades, and environments!

What is Escape Road 2

Escape Road 2 is the second version of the famous Escape Road game that brings players to an endless chase from police officers. However, this chase will be much more intense and dramatic than the original version. The police have been improved to be faster and smarter to chase you. They are more determined, have better weapons, and employ more sophisticated tactics. It's a driving game that puts players behind the wheel in a thrilling race against the police. Master high-speed maneuvers, outsmart relentless chasers, unlock new powerful vehicles, and escape!

How to Play

Drive, Smash, Escape

As you may anticipate from a thrilling chase game, the rules of Escape Road 2 are extremely straightforward but challenging. Your main goal is to drive, escape, and survive for as long as you can. Your pursuit will be relentless, and the power of the police cars and military helicopters will only increase.

All you must do is to drive your car at maximum speed while avoiding objects like cars and buildings. When your automobile reaches its maximum speed, it can even destroy other cars. The objective is to survive as long as possible. If the police vehicles get you, the game is over. Race against the police and take a spot on the leaderboard.

Unlock New Vehicles

Along the race, you should collect and accumulate coins. Then, you can use them to unlock a lot of amazing new cars in the shop. From Common to Rare to Epic to Legendary, there are up to 90 new cars that are just waiting for you to unlock. There are also armored tanks, race cars, luxury supercars, and much more.

New Features Added

Compared to the original version, Escape Road 2 game has been improved with many new features, promising to bring players a better experience:

  • Modern new cars with stunning graphics

  • Get out of stuck vehicles and find new ones

Game Controls

  • To start and turn: Use A/D or left/right arrows
  • To drive: Use A/S/D or Left/Down/Right arrows
  • To enter or exit a vehicle: Press the Spacebar
  • To use parachute: Press Space when in air

  • Huge Vehicle Collection: Unlock a vast array of vehicles from standard cars to luxury supercars
  • Completely Free: Play right in your web browser without any downloads

Tips and Strategies

  • Avoid congested areas where possible
  • Buy upgrades to increase your car's power
  • Make abrupt turns instead of driving straight
  • Be ready to abandon your vehicle if stuck
  • Keep collecting coins for better vehicles
