Game Instructions

About Escape Road Halloween

Escape Road Halloween is a variation of Escape Road game with a Halloween theme. The robbers will escape at night, and the Halloween atmosphere will cover the city. Experience the thrill of the chase in this spooky night-time setting.

How to Play

  • Use A/D or Left/Right arrows to steer
  • Press W or Up Arrow to accelerate
  • Press S or Down Arrow to reverse
  • Navigate through the dark city streets
  • Avoid obstacles in the Halloween night
  • Master driving in spooky conditions
