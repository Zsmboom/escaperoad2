About Ragdoll Hit Stickman

Ragdoll Hit Stickman is an action-packed fighting game with a variety of characters and weapons, promising intense battles. Play now to experience it!

How to Play

  • Use WASD or Arrow keys to move
  • Click or Space to attack
  • Choose different characters and weapons
  • Fight against opponents
  • Master combat techniques
  • Complete challenges and missions
